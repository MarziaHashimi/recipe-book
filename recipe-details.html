Chocolate Chip Cookies

Soft, chewy, and perfect for every occasion!

Home Add Recipe Recipe List

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Nutritional Information:

Component Amount per Serving
Calories 250
Fat 12g
Sugar 18g
Protein 3g

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugar together.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  4. Add flour and baking soda. Stir until combined.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Let cool and enjoy!

Vegan Lobia (Red kidney bean)

Healthy and full of flavor, perfect for a plant-based diet.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Red kidney bean (soaked overnight)
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt and chili powder to taste

Nutritional Information:

Calories Fat Carbs Protein
180 3g 28g 9g

Instructions:

  1. Boil the soaked lobia until soft.
  2. Saute onions in oil, add cumin and ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add boiled lobia and simmer for 10 mins.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve with rice or bread.

Quick Spaghetti

Ready in under 20 minutes for busy evenings!

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated parmesan (optional)

Nutritional Information:

Calories Fat Carbs Protein
250 6g 42g 7g

Instructions:

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water and drain.
  2. In a pan, saute garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add tomato sauce, oregano, salt and pepper.
  4. Mix in spaghetti and stir for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Serve hot.

Shor Nakhood

Spicy and tangy chickpeas – a favorite street food from Afghanistan.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups boiled chickpeas
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 chopped onion
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Nutritional Information:

Calories Fat Carbs Protein
160 2g 30g 8g

Instructions:

  1. Mix chickpeas with spices, lemon juice, and onions.
  2. Let sit for 10 minutes for flavor to blend.
  3. Garnish with cilantro and serve chilled or at room temp.
